    Inside The GoodPeople Data Company

    All of our products embed Data As A Service - it doesnt matter whether you use our property technology or our surveillance technology, you will have thousands of data points at your fingertips.


    The new scrapeout system


    Scrapeout uses up to 6,000 data points to link individuals together for potential conflicts of interest. What's more, the service has a "monitoring" function to ensure that your data is always up to date.

    Learn about Scrapeout          

    Verify


    Verify is a digital verification platform that allows your business to verify any person or other business in South Africa in seconds. Digital onboarding, simplified.

    Learn about Verify          

    Tenant Management System


    Our Tenant Management System has automated all vetting and onboarding for estate agents. The platform screens tenants in a seamless workflow, creating digital contracts, digital signatures and automating the first invoices. The platform has been integrated with all major property data and software providers in South Africa.

    Learn about TMS          

    Our data services are vast.

    Our data services include all major sources of data in South Africa, whether you want to screen companies or individuals.

     

    ID Verification services

    These include basic Home Affairs checks, but we've also added some really cool biometric tools that allow companies to biometrically verify any person, on any smart device with a camera from anywhere.

    Asylum Seekers and Refugees

    Verify asylum seekers and refugees online in a fast, easy to use platform. Other cool technologies allow us to screen passports and visas from more than 200 territories around the world to verify their authenticity.
